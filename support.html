About the App

Indian Movies DB is a comprehensive Indian cinema database featuring over 32,000 films across 20 languages, spanning from 1901 to the present day. Search by title, actor, director, language, genre, or year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are some movie posters not showing?

Poster images are loaded from The Movie Database (TMDB). Some films may not have poster images available on TMDB. An active internet connection is required to load poster images.

Do I need an internet connection?

The movie database is stored locally on your device, so you can search and browse films offline. However, an internet connection is needed to display poster images.

How often is the database updated?

The database is updated regularly with new releases and corrections. Updates are delivered through app updates on the App Store and Google Play.

Is the app free?

Yes. Indian Movies DB is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

What languages are covered?

The app covers films in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Odia, Assamese, Urdu, Nepali, Sanskrit, Sindhi, Kashmiri, and more.
