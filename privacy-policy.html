Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Indian Movies DB ("the App"), developed by Bollywood.com LLC ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Indian Movies DB is designed with your privacy in mind. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

Names, email addresses, or contact information; Location data; Device identifiers or advertising IDs; Usage analytics or behavioral data; Cookies or tracking technologies; Financial or payment information.

How the App Works

Indian Movies DB is a movie database application. When you search for films, all processing happens locally on your device. No personal data is sent to our servers, or any third party during normal use of the app.

Movie Poster Images

The App loads movie poster images from The Movie Database (TMDB) servers at image.tmdb.org. When poster images are displayed, your device makes requests directly to TMDB's image servers. These requests are subject to TMDB's own privacy policy, available at themoviedb.org/privacy-policy. We do not control or have access to any data TMDB may collect from these image requests.

Third-Party Services

The App uses data provided by The Movie Database (TMDB) API. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. TMDB's privacy policy governs their handling of any data related to their services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App contains no user accounts, no social features, and no mechanisms for collecting data from any user.

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All movie data is stored locally on your device as part of the app installation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
