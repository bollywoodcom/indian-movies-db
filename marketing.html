Indian Movies DB

Indian Movies DB is the most comprehensive Indian cinema database app, featuring over 32,000 films spanning 20 languages and over a century of filmmaking — from 1901 to 2031.

Whether you are a Bollywood fan, a South Indian cinema enthusiast, or a researcher exploring the history of Indian filmmaking, this app puts the entire landscape of Indian cinema at your fingertips.

Search & Discover

Search by movie title, actor, director, or crew member. Filter by language, genre, and year range. Browse movies in a poster grid view. View detailed information for every film, including cast, crew, genres, overview, release dates, and more.

20 Languages Covered

Indian Movies DB includes films in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Odia, Assamese, Urdu, Nepali, Sanskrit, Sindhi, Kashmiri, and more.

Comprehensive Indian Cinema Data

The app includes over 32,000 films and growing, with detailed cast and crew information, movie posters from TMDB, release dates, genres, and plot summaries.

Features

  • Clean and intuitive interface designed for easy browsing.
  • Search by movie title, actor, director, or crew member.
  • Filter movies by language, genre, and year range.
  • Poster grid view for visual browsing.
  • Detailed cast, crew, genre, and overview information.
  • List view with copy-all-titles feature for researchers.
  • Regularly updated database.

Free & Ad-Free

Indian Movies DB is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases. It is built for movie lovers, by movie lovers.
